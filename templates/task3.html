Instructions for Memory Sequence Game

In this game, you will test your memory skills by clicking on squares in the order they light up. The game becomes more challenging as you progress through different levels.

  • Easy Level: 3x3 grid. Memorize and click the sequence of up to 5 rounds.
  • Medium Level: 4x4 grid. The challenge increases with a larger grid.
  • Hard Level: 5x5 grid. The ultimate test of your memory!

How to play:

  • Watch the sequence carefully as the squares light up one by one.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the squares in the correct order.
  • If you make a mistake, you can try again. However, you are allowed only one mistake per level.
  • Complete all rounds in a level to progress to the next level.
  • The game ends if you exceed the allowed mistakes.

Tip: Pay close attention to the sequence and try to remember the order as the grids become more complex!
